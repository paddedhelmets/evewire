About Shopping Lists

Shopping lists track items needed to fulfill fitting specifications at specific locations.

  • Pending: Not yet started - items still need to be purchased
  • Partial: Some items have been acquired but not all
  • Complete: All required items are available at the location
  • Expired: List is past its useful date
